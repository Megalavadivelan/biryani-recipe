About biryani

Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic South Asian rice dish that has won the hearts of food lovers all around the world. It is made by layering fragrant basmati rice with tender pieces of meat, vegetables, or eggs, along with a rich blend of spices like saffron, cardamom, cloves, and cinnamon. The slow-cooking or "dum" technique allows the rice to absorb all the spices and juices, creating a perfectly balanced dish bursting with taste and aroma.
